Settings Panel with ActionPrompt

Try saying something like:

  • "Switch to dark mode", "Toggle theme"
  • "Enable notifications", "Disable notifications"
  • "Toggle autoplay", "Disable autoplay"
  • "Set quality to high", "Set quality to medium", "Set quality to low"
  • "Save settings", "Apply preferences"

Note: use natural language to interact with the settings panel.

You can use any language you want, ActionPrompt will try to understand you.
