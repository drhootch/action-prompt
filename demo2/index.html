ActionPrompt Form Demo with Chrome AI

This demo shows how to use the ActionPrompt plugin to interact with a form that can be filled out with natural language.

Try saying something like:

  • "Toggle theme", "Enable dark mode", "Switch to light mode"
  • "Hi, I'm John Smith from Canada, I'm 25 years old and love coffee"
  • "My name is Sarah Johnson, I'm 32 and I prefer tea"
  • "I am Mohamed Ali from Egypt, 28 years old, and I enjoy fresh juice"
  • "Fill the form with name Alice Brown, age 30, from Australia"

You can use any language you want, the plugin will try to understand you.
